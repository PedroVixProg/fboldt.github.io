Gestures_InertialSensors_EAAI

  • There are two data inputs in the files: the accelerometer (it was adjusted in the range of + 1.5 g) and the gyroscope (it was adjusted in the measurement range of + -110 degrees). There are 9 gestures recognized through fixed sensors, all were executed once in succession, with pause between gestures (from Gesture 1 to Gesture 9).

  • There is a video that explains those moviments: https://github.com/pinojoke/Gestures_InertialSensors_EAAI/commit/83846ccf89720ff86269f5bf30f573041f7eeffb

  • To read the data and understand: https://www.sparkfun.com/datasheets/Accelerometer/MMA7260Q-Rev1.pdf and http://store.invensense.com/datasheets/invensense/PS-IXZ- 0500B-00-03.pdf

  • The data is in a comma-separated format (.CSV), where each column contains the following data (from left to right): AccelerometerWrist_X, AccelerometerWrist_Y, AccelerometerWrist_Z, GyroscopeWrist_X, GyroscopeWrist_Z, AccelerometerFinger_X, AccelerometerFinger_Y, GycelerometerFinger_Z, GycelerFinger_Z.

  • Each line represents an instance of the measurement time. During a measurement (recorded in form file assuntoXX_measurementYY).
